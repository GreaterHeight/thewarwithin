The Doctrine

Every crisis visible in the world today is a projection of a crisis unresolved within the people who inhabit it.

This is not a self-help book. It is a campaign manual — a structured doctrine for navigating the seventeen battles that rage inside every human being who refuses to let their potential die quietly in the distance between who they are and who they were made to be. Named. Mapped. Fought in sequence. Won by the person who decides the war is worth entering.
