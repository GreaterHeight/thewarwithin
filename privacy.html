Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how TECHSPIBUS Media and Publishing ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects personal information in connection with the website thewarwithin.you and its associated content. We operate from Lagos, Nigeria, and serve a global audience. This policy is drafted to comply with the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDPA), the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation 2019 (NDPR), and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

By using this site, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the site.

What We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Contact information: name and email address provided when you subscribe to War Letters, join the movement, or submit an order enquiry.
  • Usage data: pages visited, time spent, scroll depth, and click patterns — collected automatically via Google Analytics 4 and Microsoft Clarity, only with your consent.
  • Device and browser data: browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and approximate location (country/region level) — collected automatically on analytics consent.
  • Communications: messages sent to us via email or contact forms, retained for the purpose of responding.

We do not collect payment card data directly. Orders are processed through third-party platforms (e.g. Selar) under their own privacy policies.

Cookies and Analytics

This site uses cookies and similar tracking technologies. We use a consent management system that allows you to control which non-essential cookies are active. You can change your preferences at any time via the "Cookie Preferences" button on every page.

Necessary cookies are required for the site to function correctly and cannot be disabled.

Analytics cookies (Google Analytics 4, measurement ID G-39E56ELNQ5; Microsoft Clarity, project ID x5hzkv6naq) help us understand how visitors engage with the campaign. These are only activated with your explicit consent. IP addresses are anonymised. We do not use analytics data for advertising and do not sell it to third parties.

For more information on how Google handles data, see Google's Privacy Policy. For Microsoft Clarity, see Microsoft's Privacy Statement.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR / NDPA)

We process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Consent — for analytics cookies and email communications you opt into. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legitimate interests — for site security, fraud prevention, and improving our services, balanced against your rights.
  • Contract performance — for processing enquiries and orders you initiate.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To send War Letters and campaign communications you have subscribed to
  • To respond to order enquiries and support requests
  • To understand how visitors engage with the site and improve content
  • To maintain site security and prevent abuse

We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

Your Rights (GDPR / NDPA 2023 / NDPR)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: request deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances
  • Restriction: request that we limit processing of your data
  • Portability: receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdrawal of consent: withdraw consent for analytics at any time via Cookie Preferences

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@techspibus.com. We will respond within 30 days. Nigerian residents may also file complaints with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC).

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected. Email subscriber data is retained until you unsubscribe. Analytics data is retained per the default retention settings of Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity. Communications are retained for a reasonable period to maintain our records.

Third-Party Services

This site uses the following third-party services that may process personal data under their own policies: Google Analytics 4 (analytics), Microsoft Clarity (session recording), Google Fonts (typography), and Selar (order processing). We recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please contact us promptly if you suspect any unauthorised use of your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when changes were last made. Continued use of the site after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
