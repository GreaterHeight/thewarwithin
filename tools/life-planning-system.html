Life Planning System

Vision · Strategy · Campaign · Operations
Every daily action must be traceable back to its original mandate.
What This Instrument Is

The Life Planning System operates across four time horizons — Vision (10 years), Strategy (3 years), Campaign (12 months), and Operations (90 days) — ensuring that every decision you make is connected to the assignment you were built for.

Build it once at the beginning of the campaign. Review quarterly. Rebuild annually. Every subsequent battle is executed within the framework this instrument establishes.

What This Instrument Contains
Horizon 1
Vision (10 Years)
The full expression of your assignment. Written in present tense as though looking back from ten years ahead.
Horizon 2
Strategy (3 Years)
What must be built or established in three years to make the 10-year vision attainable.
Horizon 3
Campaign (12 Months)
The primary campaign for this year — name, primary objective, three supporting objectives, success criteria.
Horizon 4
Operations (90 Days)
Specific actions, milestones, and non-negotiable weekly habits for the current 90-day window.
Deploy This Weapon

Download · Print · Activate

6-page instrument. One page per horizon with guided questions, plus a quarterly alignment check page. A weapon that is not deployed changes nothing. Download the instrument. Print it. Open the first page tonight.
